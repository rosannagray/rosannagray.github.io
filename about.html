Rosanna Fong, Media Designer and Superhero.

I’m quick to learn new skills, familiar with design practices, and have a reasonably sound knowledge in working with software.

I was born in Auckland, but have lived in New Plymouth for about 10 years. Now I reside in the windy city of Wellington.

I have a Bachelors degree in Design Innovation, and also a Diploma in Science and Technology. I like to combine skills and knowledge acquired from both qualifications to create something beautiful on the web.

Aside from that, I’m a total foody. I love cooking, and I can bake pretty awesome cakes.

My Design Manifesto

Collect inspiration. Originality is important. Listen to what others have to say. Love what you do. Experiment with new concepts. Catergorise your ideas. Tell a story.

Grow your ideas. Respond to challenges. Organise and plan. Waste as little as possible.
