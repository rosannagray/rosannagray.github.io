About Me

Hey there! My name's Rosanna. I have a passion for web and graphic design. I love what I do as a digital media designer and I hope to pursue a career in web design.

I've graduated from Victoria University of Wellington with a Bachelors degree in Design Innovation with a major in media design. After graduating in 2013, I have been involved in some small-scaled design projects as a freelancer, and have been temping for an agency for a short time.

Aside from that, I like playing Dominion, tinkering with my Warhammer models, sketching, and experimenting with new cooking recipes.
