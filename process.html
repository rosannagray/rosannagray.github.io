My Process

Research

This stage involves identifying the target audience, finding inspiration from other designers, and pin-pointing the main objective of the project.

Brainstorm

A brainstorm can help map out some of the ideas collected from the research. Ideas can be categorised at this stage.

Conceptualise

Ideas are sketched out from the brainstorm. As with most of my previous design projects, a sharpie and paper can take me a long way when conceptualising ideas. Draft layouts are drawn at this stage too.

Plan

Making a plan can help with time management. Requirements and specifications of the project are established at this stage. Planning for possible challenges can also be beneficial.

Develop

The nuts and bolts of the project are worked on here. This is where I make use of my technical and problem-solving skills to bring the design to life.

Polish

Whenever there's a bit of extra time, I like to add a bit of polish to my work.
