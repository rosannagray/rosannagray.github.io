The Learning Centre and Whānau Family Support Services Trust Website

Providing information about learning services.

The Learning Centre and Whānau Family Support Services Trust is part of Literacy Aotearoa and provides learning services to the community. In 2013, I redesigned their website with a new look and updated content.

About the project

The brief

To redesign the website for The Learning Centre and Whānau Family Support Services Trust.

The challenge

Create a custom website for The Learning Centre and Whānau Family Support Services Trust using both their brand and their parent organisation's brand. See Literacy Aotearoa . This link will take you to an external website..

The result

This site was launched in 2013. However, this site is no longer available as The Learning Centre and Whānau Family Support Services Trust have integrated with their parent organisation's website Literacy Aotearoa . This link will take you to an external website..
