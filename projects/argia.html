Argia

An environment generated in Maya using Python script. The envrionment is my interpretation of Argia from Italo Calvino's Invisible cities This link will take you to an external website.

In the piece Chapter 8: Cities and the Dead 4 - Argia, Calvino gives the impression of a dirty city. Its inhabitants are suffocating in the clay-packed rooms, and from the dirt-filled air. Argia is not an attractive city. It is grotesque.
