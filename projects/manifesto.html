Manifesto

Collect

Collect Inspiration
Find precedents. Look out for things that captures your attention. Find things you enjoy.
Originality is important.
Nothing better than to have original content. Don't make it too awkward though. Be creative.
Listen to what others have to say.
It's not about a single idea to design something. Collect feedback. Collect a range of ideas. Be open minded.
Love what you do.
Feel inspired. Be happy with your work.
Experiment with new concepts.
Always better to try than not to try new things. Create prototypes.
Catgorise your ideas.
Reserve some quiet time to gather your thoughts. Sort your ideas. Don't design at random.
Tell a story.
Establish a narrative. Your story can generate a theme.

Grow

Grow your ideas.
Don't be stuck at the starting point of an idea. Keep branching it out. Grow it over time.
Respond to challenges.
Be ready to solve unexpected challenges. Be flexible.
Organise and plan.
Plan your time. Plan your tools. Be realistic. Save time.
Waste as little as possible.
Think about reusable assets. An asset can be designed to fit into different contextual settings.
