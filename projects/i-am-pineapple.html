The brief

To customise a theme for an ecommerce website using Shopify.

The challenges

Coming up with an idea for the website and finding content for the website.

Dissecting how Liquid templates work.

The result

I've spent some time looking at a variety of Stock images to get inspired and ended up finding lots of pineapple images that are free for commercial use with no attribution required. Thanks to sites like pixabay.com . This link will take you to an external website. and pexels.com . This link will take you to an external website..

I then had plenty of fun playing around with Liquid templates to customise the default Shopify theme. The default theme installed was Debut.

The website was launched recently. You can see it at
