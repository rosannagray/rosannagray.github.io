About the project

The brief

To create a basic drawing application.

The challenge

To have the application work on multiple devices.

The result

The My Doodler application is still in its early stages, but is at a point where users can play around with some of its features. At the moment, users can:

  • Draw an image on the canvas.
  • Add a drawing to a collection.
  • Clear the canvas.
  • View collection of drawings.

I am still in the process of improving and adding features to the application. Please also note that the application cannot store data yet and will lose content when a user refreshes their browser.

If you want a play with My Doodler, check it out at rosannafong.github.io/my-doodler . This link will take you to an external website..
