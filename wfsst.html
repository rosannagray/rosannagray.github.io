About the Project

A website done for The Learning Center & Whānau Family Support Services Trust.

The aim is to promote their presence in Lower Hutt as well as giving the general public information about their courses and services.

The date for the publication of their website has not been confirmed yet. However, their website can be viewed at http://preview.rosanna.co.nz/.

Desktop version of their website built using Bootstrap 3.0.

Responsive layouts have been designed for The Learning Center & Whānau Family Support Services Trust's website as well. Their website can be view using tablet and mobile devices too.
